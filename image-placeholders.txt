Portfolio Website Image Placeholders

You can use these free placeholder image services to add temporary images to your portfolio:

1. For profile image (profile.jpg):
   - https://placehold.co/400x400?text=Your+Name
   - Or use a photo of yourself

2. For project images (project1.jpg, project2.jpg, project3.jpg):
   - https://placehold.co/800x500?text=Project+1
   - https://placehold.co/800x500?text=Project+2
   - https://placehold.co/800x500?text=Project+3

When you have actual project screenshots or images, remember to replace these placeholders.
